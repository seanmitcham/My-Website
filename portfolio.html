Portfolio

Below are organizations, experiences, and activities that have shaped my personal and professional growth.

Natchitoches Regional Medical Center (NRMC) — IT Intern

As an IT Intern at Natchitoches Regional Medical Center, I provide technical support to hospital staff by troubleshooting hardware and software issues, responding to help desk tickets, and setting up computers and other devices for employees. I also assist with maintaining network connectivity, installing and updating software, managing user accounts, and ensuring hospital technology operates efficiently to support patient care.

Skills Developed: Help desk and technical support, hardware installation and configuration, network troubleshooting, Active Directory and user account management, customer service and communication, problem-solving in a fast-paced environment, teamwork and professionalism.

How it contributed to my growth: This internship has strengthened my problem-solving, communication, and technical skills while giving me valuable real-world experience in a professional healthcare IT environment.

Sigma Nu Fraternity — President

As President of my Sigma Nu chapter, I've served in multiple executive positions, promoting brotherhood, service, and academic excellence through campus involvement.

How it contributed to my growth: This role has given me hands-on leadership experience, taught me accountability and teamwork, and helped me build a strong professional network.

Northwestern State Golf Club — Co-Founder / Vice President

I helped organize and manage the university's first student golf club, balancing leadership responsibilities with academic and extracurricular commitments.

How it contributed to my growth: Building the club from the ground up strengthened my organizational and time management skills, and gave me experience taking a project from idea to reality.

Reginald F. Lewis Scholars Program (Cohort 4) — Scholar

This program recruits Black male students and provides educational programs, mentoring opportunities, and co-curricular experiences to improve outcomes.

How it contributed to my growth: Being part of this cohort has connected me with mentors and professional development opportunities that are preparing me for success after graduation.

Sorbonne University Presentation

I presented "The Role of Artificial Intelligence in Modern Education" at Sorbonne University in Paris, France, in October 2025.

How it contributed to my growth: This experience helped me develop public speaking and research skills, and gave me the opportunity to share ideas with an international academic audience.
