Goals and Objectives

Short-Term Goals

  • Graduate from Northwestern State University with degrees in Computer Information Systems and Business Administration.
  • Gain more hands-on experience through my IT internship at Natchitoches Regional Medical Center (NRMC).
  • Continue developing leadership skills as President of my Sigma Nu chapter while maintaining a strong GPA.

Long-Term Goals

  • Build a successful career in cybersecurity or information technology management.
  • Earn advanced IT certifications and eventually move into a leadership role within the technology field.
  • Achieve financial stability by owning a home, investing consistently, and eventually starting my own business.

Reflection

Several support systems are helping me work toward these goals. Northwestern State University provides the education, faculty support, and opportunities I need to build both the technical and business knowledge required for my career. Sigma Nu has given me leadership experience as President of my chapter, along with lessons in accountability, teamwork, and a strong professional network I can rely on going forward. The Reginald F. Lewis Scholars Program connects me with mentors and professional development opportunities that are preparing me for success after graduation. Finally, my IT internship at Natchitoches Regional Medical Center allows me to apply what I've learned in the classroom to real-world problems, helping me build the practical experience I'll need for a full-time career in IT.
